HOW TO REACH US

At Evergreen Hospital, we are committed to providing exceptional care and fostering a relationship built on trust, compassion, and open communication. We understand that reaching the right person or department quickly is important, and we are here to assist you every step of the way. Whether you have questions about our medical services, need assistance with scheduling an appointment, or wish to speak to one of our specialists, we offer several ways to connect with us. You can contact our main line at 075-8690018 from Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and Saturday, 9:00 AM to 2:00 PM. Our friendly staff is ready to answer your calls and guide you to the right department. For non-urgent matters or general inquiries, please email us at contact@evergreenhospital.com. Our team is committed to responding promptly to all emails, ensuring you receive the information you need without delay. You can also visit us in person at 123 Evergreen Way, City, State, ZIP Code. Our hospital is conveniently located and easily accessible, with ample parking for visitors. We strive to make every interaction with our team as comfortable and helpful as possible because your health and satisfaction are our top priorities.





EMERGENCY CONTACTS

At Evergreen Hospital, we are dedicated to not only providing exceptional medical care but also ensuring you feel safe, supported, and heard. In the event of a medical emergency, it is vital to contact emergency services immediately by dialing 911. Additionally, if you require urgent assistance with poison exposure, our Poison Control helpline is available at 031-2345890. For those facing a mental health crisis or needing emotional support, we encourage you to reach out to our mental health hotline at 0214566543. Beyond emergencies, we believe in continuous improvement and greatly value your feedback. Sharing your experiences helps us enhance the quality of care and services we provide. We invite you to use the form below to provide feedback, share your thoughts, or suggest ways we can better meet your needs. Your insights are crucial to our mission of delivering compassionate and personalized care to every individual who walks through our doors. We look forward to hearing from you and thank you for trusting Evergreen Hospital with your health and well-being.
